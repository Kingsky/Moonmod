How to get there

To get to The Moon you have to build a portal. The portal looks same as Nether portal, but is made from cheese, because as you Moon is made from cheese, isn’t it? To activate the portal use Sonic Screwdiver. As you find out later the cheese myth isn’t really true, but every there is a little bit of truth in every tale.

Living on The Moon On Moon there are few changes against Minecraftia. As you know, Moon doesn’t have an atmosphere. So you have to wear super-duper Moon Suit or you will suffocate in no time. Also because of absence of atmosphere you cannot place certain blocks including plants, fluids and fire operating crafts (furnaces, torches). If you want to smelt ores or use torches. No problem. Just take a piece of Minecraftia with you. It’s really easy, just craft a Moon Tent for you. Tent unites a protection and portability. You want to move on? Just pack your tent and head to adventure.

The Moon has a lower gravity than Minecraftia. So you can jump higher and fall deeper at ease. But this could be a little contra productive. So if you want to jump a little use duck jump i.e. jump while sneaking.

FAQ

Q: How do I build the portal?

A: First create a vertical frame 4 by 5 from cheese. Then right click to activate it with Sonic Screwdriver.

Q: How do I build a tent? It says "Couldnt build a tent." What should I do? A: Well placing a tent is a bit tricky. It dont works right now but normally, you have to make sure you have a flat area 8 by 8 block. Above these platform there have to be at least 4 block of free space (air). Then dig a hole in the middle of this platform and fill it with tent block. This flat platform will be repalced with tent floor.

    Reinforced Cloth

    Top Minecraft scientist copied this technology directly from NASA, so it has to work, sure? It’s like the ordinary cloth but it’s reinforced with golden coating, which provides airtightness and blocks cosmic rays.

    Moon Tent

    Your very own piece of homeland in vast nothingness of Moon. To build one you will need a Tent Block, Sonic Screwdriver and flat solid area. First dig out one block and replace it with Tent Block, then right click while holding charged Sonic Screwdriver and voila you have your Moon Tent ready. Inside the tent same rules as on the surface apply. You don’t have to wear Moon Suit. You can place all blocks (except Bed) use torches and so on. If you want to move on, simply click the Tent Block again and Moon Tent will pack into small carry able package.

    Moon Lamp

    On Moon it’s kinda dark, but ordinary torches don’t work here. Problem? Not really, just use these Moon Lamps!

    Moon Suit

    This piece of clothing is the most important part of your wardrobe. It protects you on surface of the moon. It provides same protection as leather armour so take care of it, but it grants you ability to breath in vacuum on Moon surface.

    Sonic Screwdriver

    This is the most important tool for Moon explorer. It has 30 uses before it turns off. To make it operative again you have to recharge it. Note: You can only recharge a fully depleted screwdriver.
